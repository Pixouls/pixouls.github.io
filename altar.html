Trans is not a universal term, both across space and history. It is a word that captures an experience of flux.

These deities do not follow the conventions of gender and/or gender expressions of the mortals.

Submissions for this list were crowd sourced by friends.

The Deities

Babaylan

Origin: Central and Southern Philippines
Mythic identity: Spiritual Shamans

Note: Babaylan shamanic practices continue to today.

Dionysus-Bacchus

Origin: Ancient Greece
Mythic identity: God of Grape-Harvest, Wine-making and Wine, Homosexuality and Effeminacy

Hermaphroditus

Origin: Ancient Greece
Mythic identity: God of Hermaphrodites and Effeminacy

Kuan Yin

Origin: Ancient China (Buddhist)
Mythic identity: God of Mercy, Kindness, and Compassion

Ne Zha

Origin: Ancient China
Mythic identity: Marshal of the Central Altar, Third Lotus Prince, God of Teenagers and Professional Drivers

Oshumare

Origin: Southwestern Nigeria (Yoruba people)
Mythic identity: Òrìṣà, Serpent-Rainbow

Tu Er Shen

Origin: Ancient China
Mythic identity: The Leveret Spirit, Rabbit God, Patron of Homosexuality
