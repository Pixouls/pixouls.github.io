Beyond Gender: Mythic Dieties

★A Micro Altar★★★


Trans is not a universal term, both across space and history. It is a word that captures an experience of flux.

These dieties do not follow the conventions of gender and/or gender expressions of the mortals.

Submissions for this list were crowdsourced by friends.


This webpage was made by Pixel in SeaMonkey as a part of Sunday Sites on February 21, 2021.

The Dieties

Babaylan

Orgin: Central and Southern Philippines
Mythic identity: Spiritual Shamans

Note: Babaylan shamanic practices continue to today.

Dionysus-Bacchus

Orgin: Ancient Greece
Mythic identity: God of Grape-Harvest, Winemaking and Wine, Homosexuality and Effeminancy

Hermaphroditus

Orgin: Ancient Greece
Mythic identity: God of Hermaphrodites and Effeminacy

Kuan Yin

Orgin: Ancient China (Buddhist)
Mythic identity: God of Mercy, Kindness, and Compassion

Ne Zha

Orgin: Ancient China
Mythic identity: Marshal of the Central Altar, Third Lotus Prince, God of Teenagers and Professional Drivers

Oshumare

Orgin: Southwestern Nigeria (Yoruba people)
Mythic identity: Òrìṣà, Serpent-Rainbow

Tu Er Shen

Orgin: Ancient China
Mythic identity: The Leveret Spirit, Rabbit God, Patron of Homosexuality
