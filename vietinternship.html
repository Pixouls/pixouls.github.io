Vietnam Internship in Development and Gender

Howdy! If you're on this page, you're likely from futureland, or someone else from somewhere on the internet wondering what my internship is. If so, you're in the right place! This summer, from June 14 to July 23rd 2021, I will be interning with the grassroots organization ICS Center of Vietnam and participating on weekly seminars on Thursday at 11 PM EST / Friday at 10 AM ICT. This program is conducted through the School for International Training. This has been made possible through the Benjamin A. Gilman International Scholarship Program. If you are interested in learning about this scholarship program, please feel free to reach out! I will talk about my day to day experiences on my futureland daily journal. Weekly blogs will be shared through my college's Office of Off-Campus Study. Those two sources will be the best for keeping up with what I'm up to. At the end of the program, I'll write a 7-10 paper about my experience. If you haven't been on my site before, consider poking around a bit, it'll likely give you an idea about why I pursued this program as well!
