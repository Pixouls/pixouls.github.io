This encompasses a list of activities I've learned to check in with myself in regards to as someone who often finds myself at low capacity and low functioning. I come back to this list about every three weeks using a notificiation set to my calendar. Some aspects have become integrated into my routine once I was ready for them. Some of them aren't routine cases, but are still things that I wanted to get checked in on and reminded of. They are suggestions, check-in questions, and affirmations.

This page stands purely as a place for you to feel the satisfaction of checking boxes. No memory will be saved. When you refresh, it's gone. Leave it up as you try to go down the list. Or take a quick look, pick one, and go about your day.

Learning how to be a person is hard. You aren't alone.
